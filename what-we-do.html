What we do

Listing Creation

Efficient, appealing pictures are critical. Our photographer captures and showcases your home in its best point of view.
Then we put our brains to work and come up with a title and description that stands out from the rest.

Pricing Optimisation

We don’t just set a price and leave it. We take into account the season and local events. Then every couple of days we review the pricing on each channel. This way we ensure no money is left on the table.

Cleaning and Linen

We respect our cleaners and pay them fairly. In return they do a valuable job day after day. We order fresh linen and towels before every check-in from a company we trust. The cleaning and linen costs are covered by the guest. We never leave the guest without toilet rolls, shampoo, shower gel, etc. The little things matter.

Guest Screening

We check past reviews. We check profile verifications. We ask questions. Each guest has to sign an agreement and send a photo ID. Most guests are genuine, we keep out the bad ones.

Guest Communication

We deal with every guest communication from enquiry to check-out.
We have someone available at the computer each and every day 8:00-22:00. We always provide an emergency phone number the guest can call outside of these hours.
Which we actually pick up.

24 hour check-in

Guests can check-in anytime they want to. Flexibility is crucial, the reviews always reflect if a check-in process is not smooth enough.

Maintenance

Lightbulb change? Door handle loose? All taken care of.
In case there is a bigger issue where a specialist is needed, you are the first person to be notified. Managing these tasks depends on our agreement according to your preferences.

Property Check

Our cleaners have a general checklist they always go through after every guest. We can also add extra items to check unique to your property according to your request.
